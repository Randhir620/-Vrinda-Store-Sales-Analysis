# 📊 Vrinda Store Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes the sales performance of Vrinda Store for the year 2022 using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations, charts, and pivot tables.

The objective is to identify sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, top-performing states, order status distribution, and sales channel performance to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement
Businesses generate large volumes of sales data, but extracting meaningful insights from this data can be challenging. This project aims to analyze sales records and provide a clear understanding of:

- Monthly sales trends
- Customer demographics
- Sales channel performance
- Product demand patterns
- Regional sales performance

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze sales and order performance.
- Identify customer buying behavior.
- Compare monthly sales and order trends.
- Find top-performing states.
- Evaluate sales channel contribution.
- Generate actionable business insights.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 1. Orders vs Sales Analysis
- Monthly comparison of total sales and order count.
- Trend identification throughout the year.

### 2. Sales: Men vs Women
- Gender-wise contribution to total sales.
- Women contributed approximately 65% of total sales.

### 3. Orders: Age vs Gender
- Analysis of purchasing behavior across age groups.
- Adult customers generated the highest number of orders.

### 4. Top 5 States by Sales
- Maharashtra
- Karnataka
- Uttar Pradesh
- Telangana
- Tamil Nadu

### 5. Order Status Analysis
- Delivered Orders
- Returned Orders
- Refunded Orders
- Cancelled Orders

### 6. Sales Channel Performance
- Amazon
- Flipkart
- Myntra
- Ajio
- Meesho
- Nalli
- Others

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

✅ March recorded the highest sales.

✅ Women contributed nearly 65% of total revenue.

✅ Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh were the top-performing states.

✅ Around 92% of orders were successfully delivered.

✅ Amazon was the highest-performing sales channel.

✅ Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra together contributed nearly 80% of total sales.

---

## 📊 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing campaigns on female customers.
- Increase inventory planning before March peak season.
- Strengthen operations in top-performing states.
- Expand partnerships with high-performing marketplaces.
- Improve return and cancellation management.
